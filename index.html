An early start.

We had a long day ahead of us. A ground transport vehicle (GTV) came to pick us for breakfast. That's me with my back to the camera stepping onto one of the many GTV we would use that day.

    During breakfast they gave us our itinerary.
  1. Practice collecting a soil sample
  2. Liftoff – 0900 Hours
  3. Lunch on the moon
  4. Collect a soil sample
  5. Salute American Flag for PR photo
  6. Return home
